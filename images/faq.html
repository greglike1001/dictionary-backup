FAQs

Ku2En.com is the world's first Kurdish to English; English to Kurdish dictionary that learns along with you! Our goal is to assist students of the Kurdish and English languages to improve their understanding and assist others in mastering each language. Please read the guide below to learn more.

How does the dictionary work?

Like any traditional dictionary, Ku2En.com works by presenting the English definition of Kurdish words.

You may search for any definition using the English, Kurdish latin or Kurdish Sorani equivalent word.

For example, to find the definition of happy, you may search for any of the following:

  • happy
  • khoshḥal
  • خشحال

How does Ku2En.com learn with me?

Although we have collected a large number of definitions, our work is not complete, and we would love your assistance to help our dictionary grow.

If you are an advanced or native speaker, you may contribute to our dictionary by submitting a missing definition

Simply search for any word, and if not found, you will be prompted to assist other learners by contributing the word and definition. Please remember to include your location as many words may be regionally-specific.

You may also help us by revising erroneous words

How Can I revise an existing definition?

To revise an erroneous definition, simply search for the word and click "revise this definition".

We'll do our homework and make sure your revision is accurate.
