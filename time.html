Time and Directions

22 Nov, 2015 Kurdish Grammar 3 Comments 7

Eating

The following is a conversation between two strangers.

John: katzhmir chanda?/saat chanda?
Hello, what is the time?

Atta: Saat yaki dwanewaroya
The time is One O'clock in the afternoon

John: Supas, Taxer bum/drang kawtm. Garaje pasakan la kuya?
Thank you, I am running late. Where is the bus station?

Atta: Garaji pasakan akaweta bashuri bazarakawa, la kotay shaqamaka bsuryrawa ba dasa chap dwatr rek bro.
The bus station is South of the Souk. Turn left at the end of the street and head straight.

John: Saat chand pasaka darwa/arwa
Thank you. What time does the bus leave?

Atta: pasaka dawa la du xulak ballam Yakike ka haya la penj xulak
The bus leaves in 10 minutes, but there will be another in 5 minutes.

John: Supas, Abyt palabkam ta fryay pasaka bkawm la yakw da daqa
Thank you, I will hurry and catch the bus at 10 past 1.

Atta: ama rya xyratra. dasta rasta bsuryrawa la ama shaqama wu bro bo penj xulak
This route is quicker. Turn right at this street and walk for 5 minutes

John: Supas. Xwet Lagall.
Thank you. Goodbye.
