Comparative and Superlative Adjectives

13 Dec, 2015 Kurdish Grammar 0 Comments 11

The comparative and superlative refers to means of comparison.

Rule:

The comparative and superlative include:

  • -tir: comparative -er
  • la: (comparison)
  • -tirin: superlative -est (precedes the nouns they modify).

Examples:

  • gawra - big
  • gawratir - bigger
  • garm - warm
  • garmtir - warmer
  • zya - much
  • zyatir - more

  • Amro la dwene sardtir a - Today is colder than yesterday
  • Ama l'awa chaktir a - much

  • sardtirin rozh - The coldest day
  • chaktirin shitan - The best things
  • jwantirin mindal - The most beautiful child
